City Palace – Jaipur 🏯

City Palace is a royal residence that still houses the Jaipur royal family. It’s a blend of Rajput, Mughal, and European architecture, offering museums, courtyards, and gardens.

Highlights:

  • Chandra Mahal
  • Mubarak Mahal
  • Royal artifact museums

Fun Fact: Some parts of the palace are still the official residence of the royal family.

Visitor Info:

  • Entry fee: ₹150 for Indian visitors, ₹700 for foreigners
  • Timings: 9:30 AM – 5:00 PM
  • Guided tours available for deeper history
"City Palace reflects Jaipur’s rich royal heritage and grandeur."
