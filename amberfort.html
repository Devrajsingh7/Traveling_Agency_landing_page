Amber Fort 🏰

Amber Fort is a magnificent fort-palace built of red sandstone and marble, overlooking Maota Lake. It blends Rajput and Mughal architectural styles.

Highlights:

  • Sheesh Mahal (Mirror Palace)
  • Diwan-i-Aam (Hall of Public Audience)
  • Ganesh Pol Gate

Fun Fact: The fort has an underground passage used by the royal family in emergencies.

Visitor Info:

  • Entry fee: ₹100 for Indian visitors, ₹500 for foreigners
  • Timings: 8:00 AM – 5:30 PM
  • Optional elephant rides to the fort entrance
"Amber Fort’s architecture is a perfect blend of beauty, strength, and culture."
